Web Platforms & Systems

The following are custom platforms I designed, developed, and shipped — most built solo as the sole developer.

Segura / Viver Fragrância — Omni Accounts Integration

WHAT IT IS A custom accounting automation layer built for Segura and Viver Fragrância, integrated with Omni Accounts — Namibia's leading business accounting software. WHAT IT DOES — Bridges e-commerce and sales data directly into Omni Accounts, eliminating manual data entry entirely — Automates invoice generation, stock updates, and financial record-keeping in real time — Built as a licensable solution — currently being packaged and sold to other businesses seeking Omni Accounts automation WHY IT MATTERS This is the first integration of its kind for Omni Accounts in this region. The system solves a manual accounting bottleneck that affects hundreds of businesses using this software, and opens a scalable revenue stream as a standalone SaaS product.

Seatme — Event Ticketing & Management Platform

WHAT IT IS A full-stack event ticketing and management platform built from the ground up using WordPress, PHP, and JavaScript. WHAT IT DOES — End-to-end event lifecycle management: creation, promotion, ticket sales, and access control — Real-time QR code scanning for venue access — Financial reporting, statistics dashboards, and database management tools — Promotions engine, RSVP functionality, and custom access control tiers

Bulletin — Radio Station News Platform

WHAT IT IS An online news and content management platform built specifically for radio stations. WHAT IT DOES — Bilingual interface (English and Afrikaans) for news article browsing and management — Self-read bulletin creation and editing tools for on-air presenters — One-click PDF export and email distribution of bulletins IMPACT Streamlines the daily content workflow for radio teams, reducing bulletin preparation time and eliminating the need for separate document tools.

ATTOmgee / TygerOmgee — Non-Profit Compliance Platform

WHAT IT IS A custom compliance and donation management platform built for two South African non-profit organisations. WHAT IT DOES — Automated generation of SARS-compliant Section 18A tax certificates — Exports donation records in the exact format required by SARS for tax submissions — Integrates with each organisation's online donation platform to trigger automatic certificate generation on receipt of a donation IMPACT Turned a labour-intensive manual compliance process — previously requiring staff hours per certificate — into a fully automated, single-click workflow. Reduces human error and ensures tax compliance without administrative overhead.

Doc Scientia — Bulk Order Discount Platform

WHAT IT IS A custom B2B e-commerce and quoting platform for an educational publisher, serving schools and bookstores. WHAT IT DOES — Dynamic pricing engine applies tiered bulk discounts based on order size and customer type — Automated shipping calculation based on order weight and destination — Generates professional quotes for review before order confirmation VALUE Eliminated a manual quoting process and enabled the client to scale their wholesale channel without additional admin staff.

Marafiki Game Lodge — Custom Booking System

WHAT IT DOES — Full control over bookings, real-time availability, and accommodation unit management — Integrates with external booking platforms while giving the client a unified internal dashboard — Designed for non-technical staff — minimal training required

Salon Recruiter — Beauty Industry Recruitment Platform
