Websites

A selection of website systems, platforms and custom development projects I have worked on.

Seatme

I developed a ticketing platform called Seatme. This platform was created with WordPress and custom development in PHP and JavaScript. It includes event creation, statistics, financial reports, database tools, promotions, RSVP functionality and custom access control scanning.

Bulletin

An online news platform created for radio stations. Users can browse subscribed news articles, work in two languages, create bulletin/self-read documents, modify them, email them or export them to PDF.

ATTOmgee / TygerOmgee

A custom system for two non-profit organisations to issue 18A certificates, export donation records in the required SARS format, and integrate with their online donation platforms to generate automatic certificates.

Doc Scientia - BOD

A bulk order discount platform for schools and bookstores. Customers can select products, receive appropriate discounts, include shipping and automatically generate a quote for review and ordering.

Marafiki

A custom booking system for a game lodge, built to integrate with booking platforms while giving the client full control over bookings, availability and accommodation options.

Salon Recruiter

A recruitment-style platform for the beauty industry, including verified candidate profiles, employer access, job listings, messaging functionality and candidate/employer matching.
