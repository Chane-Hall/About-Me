Crafts & Making

The same instinct that drives my problem-solving in code shows up in how I work with my hands. I believe creative range makes a better technologist — the ability to see a finished thing before it exists is the same skill whether you're writing code or building something physical.

My craft practice spans sewing and crochet, jewellery making, painting, ceramic sculpture, and mosaic work. I also designed and managed the physical Kabous die Kabouter character and decor installations for the Atterbury Theatre Christmas Market.
